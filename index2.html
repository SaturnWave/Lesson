Use Case Name List of Related Requirements ID Actor(s) Brief Description
Register FR01 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Register" button, in response the software will present the actor with a form and the user will enter their registration details. The software will create a new user account and send a confirmation email to the actor.
Login FR02 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Login" button, in response the software will present the actor with a form and the user will enter their login details. The software will authenticate the user and grant access to the system.
Logout FR03 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Logout" button, in response the software will log out the user and end the session.
Schedule Ride FR04 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Schedule Ride" button, in response the software will present the actor with a form and the user will enter the ride details. The software will schedule the ride and send a confirmation email to the actor.
Plan Route FR05 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Plan Route" button, in response the software will present the actor with a form and the user will enter the route details. The software will plan the route and show it to the actor.
Match Ride FR06 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Match Ride" button, in response the software will match the user with other users with similar ride details and show the matches to the actor.
Provide Feedback FR07 Student, Faculty The actor will click on the "Provide Feedback" button, in response the software will present the actor with a form and the user will enter their feedback. The software will store the feedback in the system.
Monitor System FR08 Admin The actor will click on the "Monitor System" button, in response the software will present the actor with a dashboard showing various system metrics.
Generate Reports FR09 Admin The actor will click on the "Generate Reports" button, in response the software will generate a report based on the selected criteria and present it to the actor.
Manage Users FR10 Admin The actor will click on the "Manage Users" button, in response the software will present the actor with a list of users and the user will be able to perform various management actions such as adding, removing, or modifying users.
